Privacy Policy

Rythamo Day - Personal Wellness Journal

Last updated: January 14, 2026

Summary: Rythamo Day is a privacy-first journaling app. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

1. Information We Collect

Rythamo Day is designed with your privacy in mind. The app collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Journal Entries: Your daily reflections and answers to prompts
  • Mood Data: Your mood selections and tracking history
  • Streak Information: Your journaling consistency data
  • App Preferences: Theme settings, notification preferences, and avatar customization

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Display your journal entries and history within the app
  • Track your journaling streaks and mood patterns
  • Personalize your app experience with your chosen theme and avatar
  • Send local notifications (if enabled) to remind you to journal

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system. We do not:

  • Upload your data to any cloud servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Collect any personally identifiable information

4. Third-Party Services

Rythamo Day may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: To display custom typography in the app. No personal data is shared with Google.

5. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: View all your data directly within the app
  • Delete: Clear app data through your device settings or uninstall the app
  • Export: Your data remains on your device and is not externally accessible

6. Children's Privacy

Rythamo Day does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences interested in personal wellness and journaling.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
